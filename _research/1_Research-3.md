description: "In this line of research, I investigate how we can improve human cooperation in crisis situations and how insights from cooperation research can be used to promote resilience."
wip-list:
  - "Emergency Kit Cooperation: <i>As part of the Crisis Challenge of Univerity Utrecht's Studium Generale, I investigate cooperation in a public goods dilemma framed around sharing emergency kits. We investigate differences between groups of neighborhood versus fellow country members as well as differences when government plans are clear versus unclear. </i>Preregistration available at https://osf.io/w2g5p/"
  - "Everyday Small Talk and Social Health: <i>Together with the organisation Praatje, we will conduct experience sampling studies on short, spontaneous conversations with strangers and test the effectiveness of an app to improve small talk with challenges. The aim is to investigate how small talk and challenges to improve social contact can improve social health (more resilience, less loneliness, etc.). </i>"
  - "First-Party Punishment: <i>Using controlled experimental public goods games, I aim to investigate how first-party punishment, punishment of oneself, can impact cooperation. The aim is to understand how first-party solutions compare tto second- or third-party solultions and investigate implications for crisis settings.</i>"
